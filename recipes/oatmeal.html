Protein Oatmeal

Note: Not the best presentation.

This is a highly versatile dish, so macros can vary widly. The edition I will be using is ideal for cutting and has the following macros. This is best consumed as a pre-workout meal.

  • Protein: 29 g
  • Carbs: 37 g
  • Fats: ~1 g
  • Calories: 310

Ingredients

  • 1 serving of oatmeal
  • Half a serving of blueberries
  • 1 scoop of protein powder
  • Water

Steps

  1. Put ingredients in a bowl and mix
  2. Microwave
