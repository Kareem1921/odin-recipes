Egg Whites

Note: Camera fogged up.

This is a distinct take on egg whites. Has good macros though.

  • Protein: 44 g
  • Carbs: 10 g
  • Fats: 23 g
  • Calories: 335

Ingredients

  • 14 g (0.5 oz) walnuts
  • 110 g (4 oz) cottage cheese
  • 5 egg whites
  • 1 cop (around 90 g) of broccoli

Steps

  1. Heat scrambled egg whites on pan with oil
  2. Boil or steam broccoli with salt
  3. Mash walnuts
  4. Put cottage cheese on plate
  5. Apply seasonings of choice
