Walnut Chicken

Note: Used a bit too much sauce.

I could sell this recipe to resturaunts for millions but you all get to see it for free.

  • Protein: 29 g
  • Carbs: 37 g
  • Fats: ~1 g
  • Calories: 310

Ingredients

  • 224 g (0.5 lbs) chicken breast, cut
  • 28 oz of Walnuts
  • 2 servings of alfredo sauce
  • Crushed garlic
  • Minced onion
  • Cumin
  • Chilli powder
  • Italian seasonings (oregeno, etc.)
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Marinate with seasonings, preferably in olive oil
  2. Crush walnuts
  3. Put chicken, onion, garlic, and walnuts in pan
  4. Cook on medium heat
  5. When chicken is almost done, pour sauce in pan and mix with chicken
